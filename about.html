About Waziobia Fruit Shop

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Waziobia Fruit Shop started as a small roadside fruit stand in Mainland, Lagos. Our founder, Mrs. Ngozi Okafor, had a simple vision: to make fresh, nutritious fruits accessible to everyone in the community. What began with just mangoes and watermelons has grown into a beloved local business offering over 30 varieties of fruits and healthy products.

The name "Waziobia" represents the three major Nigerian languages - Hausa (Wa), Yoruba (Zio), and Igbo (Bia) - symbolizing unity and inclusiveness. We serve customers from all backgrounds and celebrate the diversity of our beautiful Lagos community.

Over the years, we have grown from a one-woman operation to a team of 12 dedicated staff members who share our passion for quality and service. We have served over 50,000 satisfied customers and continue to expand our offerings based on customer feedback and seasonal availability.

Our Mission

To promote healthy living by providing fresh, high-quality fruits and nutritious snacks at affordable prices while supporting local farmers and creating employment opportunities in our community.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the freshest produce from trusted local and international suppliers
  • Integrity: Honest business practices, transparent pricing, and fair treatment of all stakeholders
  • Community: Supporting local farmers, creating jobs, and giving back through community programs
  • Health: Promoting wellness through nutritious choices and healthy lifestyle education
  • Service: Treating every customer like family with warmth, respect, and excellent service
  • Sustainability: Minimizing waste and using eco-friendly packaging whenever possible

What Makes Us Different

  1. Daily Fresh Deliveries: We receive fresh produce every morning from our network of farms
  2. Quality Guarantee: If you're not satisfied with any purchase, we'll replace it or refund you immediately
  3. Knowledgeable Staff: Our team can help you choose the perfect fruits and provide storage tips
  4. Custom Orders: Need fruits for an event? We handle bulk orders and fruit platters
  5. Fair Prices: We keep our prices competitive without compromising on quality
